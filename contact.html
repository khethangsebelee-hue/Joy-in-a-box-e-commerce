Our Story

Joy in A Box was born from a simple belief: your home should be a sanctuary of comfort, beauty, and personal expression. Founded in 2020 by interior designer Emma Chen, we set out to curate and craft modern home décor that brings genuine joy to everyday life.

Every piece in our collection is thoughtfully selected or custom-made with premium, sustainable materials. We partner with artisans worldwide to ensure ethical production and timeless design. From made-to-order furniture to our cozy Fluff & Snuggles textiles, we pour love into every detail.

Our Promise: Sustainability, craftsmanship, and a personal touch. Because your home deserves the best.
